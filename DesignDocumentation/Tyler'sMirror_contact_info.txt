Tyler's Mirror (Adventure, Top-Down, Puzzle)

Tyler's Mirror is a Top-Down puzzle about mirror and lasers. You play as a lost spirit in an ancient corrupted ruin. After going down the mirror maze, you'll discover the truth of corruption and save this world.
